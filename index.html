Statistics

What is Statistics

Practice and study of collecting and analyzing data

Statistics helps to answer the following question:

  • Likelyhood customer to purchase a product
  • Customer preference of purchased method
  • How many occupants in a hotel
  • How to optimise occupancy
  • How many sizes of jeans need to be manufacture to fit 95% population
  • A/B test: Which ad or which web layout effective to pull in customer

Types of Statistics
  • Descriptive Statistics

    • Describe and summarize data

  • Inferential Statistics

    • Use a sample of data to make inferences about larger population

Types of data

Numeric (Quantitative)

  • Continuous (Measure)
    • Airplane speed
    • Time spent waiting in line
  • Discrete (Counted)
    • Number of pets
    • Number of packages shipped
